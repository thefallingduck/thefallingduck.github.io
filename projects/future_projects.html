Future Project Ideas

As I search for a faculty position, I am looking to develop projects that integrate recent and deep-time phenomena.:

• Environmental variability in the Cretaceous Western Interior Seaway. Evolutionary mode and tempo in eperic seas differs greatly from open-ocean environments. This may be due to paleoceanographic complexity of wide, shallow seaways. To this end, I am interested in developing annual or subannual records of oceanographic variability with latitude in the Cretaceous Western Interior Seaway. This work builds on my expertise with mollusk microanalysis.
